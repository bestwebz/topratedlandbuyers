Frequently Asked Questions About Selling Your Land Online
How Will I Receive Cash Offers To Sell My Land?

Our network of land buyers typically sends its cash offers to buy your land through email. Though, some like to send text messages or provide their offer over the phone. 

How Do I Know I Will Actually Get My Money When I Sell My Land?

In most cases, the land buyer you decide to work with will hire a title company or real estate attorney to handle the purchase and sale of your vacant land. This means that you will have a third-party working for you on your behalf. The buyer’s money is sent to an escrow account where the title company (or real estate attorney in some states). By law this money has to be sent to you once you sign the appropriate paperwork. You can call the title company or real estate attorney at anytime to ask questions. Typically, you will receive the money either as a paper check sent to you by FedEx or UPS or, if you choose, by wire transfer. 

Can I Meet the Land Buyer in Person?

This website connects 60+ companies that buy land from all across the US. Rarely do our land buyers meet property owners who want to sell land in person. 

How Fast Can I Receive Cash For My Vacant Land?

Our network of land buyers help property owners sell land for cash. They do not need to wait months for loans from the bank. The transaction can be done in as little as a few days. More commonly our land buyers complete the transaction within 3 weeks.

Do I Have to Pay for Back Taxes Owed on My Vacant Land I Want to Sell?

No. Many times, depending on the land buyer you want to work with, the land buyer will offer to pay off all back taxes and liens on your property. Plus, many land buyers will also offer to pay closing costs. This means the cash offer to sell your land you see is the amount of cash you will receive.

Can I Sell My Vacant Land Without a Real Estate Agent?

Yes, it’s 100% legal (and easy) to sell your own vacant land without a real estate agent. Many times all that is required for you to sell your land is for you and other property owners (if any) to sign a few documents in the presence of a notary. Our network of experienced land buyers specialize in making the sale of vacant land easy and hassle-free. 

Is My Personal Information Secure on This Website?

We share your property’s information with over 60 different companies that buy land to help you sell your land fast. Most of the information we collect is freely available through public records. 

What Types of Land Do Your Land Buyers Really Buy?

As real estate investors who specialize in buying vacant land, our network of our land buyers are interested in buying land that has road access and favorable attributes.
